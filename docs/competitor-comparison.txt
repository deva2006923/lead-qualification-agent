COMPETITOR COMPARISON GUIDE
===========================

Overview
--------
This document outlines how our platform compares to leading CRM and lead intelligence competitors: Salesforce Sales Cloud, HubSpot Sales Hub, and ZoomInfo.

Our Platform vs Salesforce Sales Cloud
---------------------------------------
Pricing: Our platform starts at $49/user/month (Starter), $99/user/month (Growth), and $249/user/month (Enterprise). Salesforce Sales Cloud starts at $25/user/month but core AI features require the Einstein add-on at $50/user/month, making effective cost $75+/user/month for comparable capabilities.

AI-Powered Lead Scoring: Our platform uses an on-platform ML pipeline (XGBoost + LLM explanations) with automatic retraining on feedback. Salesforce Einstein provides probabilistic scoring but explanations require additional Einstein Analytics license.

RAG-Powered Recommendations: Our platform includes built-in retrieval-augmented generation for contextual playbook suggestions. Salesforce does not offer native RAG; third-party integrations required.

Implementation Time: Our platform: 1-2 days with CSV import. Salesforce: 4-12 weeks typical enterprise rollout.

Data Portability: Our platform allows full CSV export and model artifact download. Salesforce data export is restricted and subject to API limits.

Support: Our platform offers 24/7 chat + dedicated success manager on all paid plans. Salesforce support tiers are gated behind premium support contracts.

Verdict: For mid-market companies wanting AI-first lead intelligence without enterprise implementation overhead, our platform delivers superior ROI at 40-60% lower total cost of ownership.

Our Platform vs HubSpot Sales Hub
-----------------------------------
Pricing: HubSpot Sales Hub Professional is $450/month for 5 users ($90/user/month). Enterprise is $1,200/month for 10 users. Our platform is $99/user/month Growth with no minimum seat count.

Lead Scoring: HubSpot uses rule-based predictive scoring tied to contact properties. Our platform uses ML models trained on actual conversion data, delivering 23% higher precision in head-to-head pilots.

Email Personalization: HubSpot provides templates and sequences. Our platform generates contextual draft emails grounded in case studies and playbooks specific to the prospect's industry and profile.

Integration Ecosystem: HubSpot has 1,400+ native integrations. Our platform offers REST API, Zapier, and Webhook support — sufficient for most workflows.

Reporting: HubSpot offers robust pipeline reporting. Our platform focuses on lead-level AI insights rather than pipeline aggregation.

Verdict: Companies prioritizing AI-driven prioritization over broad marketing automation should choose our platform. HubSpot remains the better choice for teams wanting an all-in-one marketing + sales suite.

Our Platform vs ZoomInfo
--------------------------
ZoomInfo is a data enrichment platform, not a scoring platform. It provides contact and firmographic data but does not score, explain, or recommend actions.

Our platform is complementary to ZoomInfo: use ZoomInfo for prospecting and list building, then import those leads into our platform for AI scoring, prioritization, and recommended outreach.

Key differentiator: ZoomInfo charges $15,000–$50,000/year for data access. Our platform works with any lead data source and adds intelligence on top of whatever data you already have.
