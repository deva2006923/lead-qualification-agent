Common Objections & Responses
-------------------------------

Objection: "Your pricing is higher than HubSpot."
Response: HubSpot Sales Hub Professional is $90/user/month and does not include ML scoring, LLM explanations, or RAG-powered recommendations. Our Growth plan at $99/user/month delivers capabilities that would cost $200+/user/month if assembled from HubSpot + Einstein + a third-party AI tool. Most customers see payback within 60 days through improved conversion rates.

Objection: "We want to try before we commit."
Response: Absolutely — that's what our 14-day free trial is for, and it requires no credit card. For Enterprise accounts, we can extend to a 30-day pilot with dedicated onboarding support.

Objection: "We're a small team, $49/user still feels like a lot."
Response: On the Starter plan, a team of 3 reps pays $147/month total. If the platform helps close even one additional deal per quarter that wouldn't have been prioritized otherwise, ROI is typically immediate. We can also discuss a month-to-month trial at standard pricing with no lock-in.

Objection: "Can we negotiate the Enterprise price?"
Response: Enterprise pricing is negotiable for multi-year commitments (2-3 year deals), large seat counts (100+), and strategic partnerships. Typical negotiable range is 10-25% off list price for qualified enterprise accounts. All negotiations go through your Account Executive.

Objection: "We already pay for Salesforce/HubSpot."
Response: Our platform integrates via CSV import today and via native API sync (Salesforce, HubSpot) on Enterprise. Many customers run us alongside their existing CRM — we handle the AI scoring and prioritization layer, while the CRM manages pipeline tracking. The net cost is incremental, but the lift in conversion rate (typically 15-30% improvement in rep efficiency) far exceeds the additional spend.

Billing & Contracts
--------------------
- Billing is monthly or annual (invoiced). Credit card or ACH for monthly; wire/ACH + Net-30 invoicing available for annual Enterprise contracts.
- Seats can be added at any time (prorated to current billing period). Seat reductions take effect at renewal.
- 30-day cancellation notice required for monthly plans. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days.
- Data export is always available regardless of plan status. We do not hold your data hostage.
